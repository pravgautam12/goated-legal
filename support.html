Common questions

Do I need an account or internet?
No. GOATED has no accounts and works fully offline, even in airplane mode. Every stat is stored right on your phone.

Why is a stat shown as a dash?
Some numbers were not tracked in a player's era (for example, blocks and steals before the 1970s). We show a dash instead of guessing a value.

How is the winner decided?
Each legend earns a GOAT Score from one fixed formula that is the same for everyone, then they are ranked all time. Tap the "How we rank" link in the app to see what goes into it.

The stats look off to me.
Numbers are sourced and double checked against reliable references. If you spot something that looks wrong, email us the player and the stat and we will review it.
