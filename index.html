Privacy Policy

The short version

GOATED does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. The app works entirely offline on your device. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no servers.

Last updated: 6/11/2026

What we collect

Nothing. GOATED has no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs that gather information about you.

How the app works

  • All player stats are bundled inside the app as a local file. Nothing is downloaded or looked up online.
  • The app makes no network requests and requires no internet connection. It runs fully in airplane mode.
  • There are no user accounts, logins, or registration. We never ask for your name, email, or any personal information.

Sharing a card

When you tap Share, GOATED creates an image on your device and hands it to your operating system's standard share sheet. Where that image goes next is entirely your choice and is handled by the other app you pick (for example, Messages or a social app). GOATED does not send the image anywhere on its own.

Children's privacy

Because GOATED collects no data whatsoever, it does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above. Since the app collects no data, any changes will be minor.
